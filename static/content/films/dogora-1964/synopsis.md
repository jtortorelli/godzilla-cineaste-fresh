---
title: Dogora
---

A large extraterrestrial creature destroys a research satellite. At the same
time, large amounts of diamonds begin disappearing around the world. Komai, a
Japanese detective, and Jackson, an insurance investigator, team up to
investigate. They discover that the same extraterrestrial creature is
responsible. The creature, named "Dogora," feeds on carbon, and has now grown to
monstrous size.

Komai and Jackson travel to Tokyo. Jackson tries to con his way into a diamond
smuggling gang, but gets himself captured. Komai tries to rescue him, but they
capture him, too. Dogora appears in Tokyo, and they escape in the confusion.
Meanwhile, the military discovers that Dogora has a strange weakness to bee
venom. They mass produce a stock of artificial bee venom for a counterattack.

The military deploys their venom weapons against Dogora. The space monster
attempts to counterattack by dividing into hundreds of smaller monsters. The
venom causes the to monsters die and crystallize, forming rocky lumps that fall
to earth. Komai and Jackson pursue the diamond smugglers along the shore. The
chase ends when falling Dogora debris cruses the smugglers. Jackson bids
farewell to Komai and goes to America to make his report.
